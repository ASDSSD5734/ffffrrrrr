About Pune

Pune is a vibrant city in Maharashtra known for its rich history, educational institutions, and cultural heritage. It is often referred to as the cultural capital of Maharashtra.

Places to Visit

Aga Khan Palace

A historical landmark associated with Mahatma Gandhi.

Sinhagad Fort

Famous fort offering trekking opportunities and panoramic views.

Raja Dinkar Kelkar Museum

A museum showcasing Indian artifacts and cultural heritage.

Agriculture

The region around Pune is known for cultivation of sugarcane, vegetables, and horticulture products. It supports both traditional and modern farming methods.

How to Reach Pune

Pune is well connected by air, rail, and road:


By Air: Pune International Airport with domestic and limited international flights.

By Train: Pune Junction railway station connects to major Indian cities.

By Road: Well connected by National Highways, buses available from nearby cities.
